The solution

One sensor in the bay. One app in the car. Zero touch.

PARQ Nodes sit flush in each bay and sense the moment a car arrives. They talk to each other to confirm the exact space, recognise the driver's app, and open a billing session. Drive away and it closes — charging the stored card to the second.

Arrive → bill
Leave → stop
Settle same-day

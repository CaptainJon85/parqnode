03 — How it works

From wheels-stopped to paid — in one continuous loop.

01
Arrival sensed
The Node's magnetometer detects the steel mass of a car settling over the bay and wakes the radios.
02
Position triangulated
Neighbouring Nodes exchange UWB time-of-flight to confirm exactly which bay is now occupied — no GPS drift.
03
Driver matched
BLE handshakes the PARQ app on the phone in that car, binding the session to the right account.
04
Billing starts
A session opens in the cloud at the zone's live tariff. The driver gets a quiet confirmation — nothing to tap.
05
Departure detected
Mass lifts, the Node confirms the bay is clear, and the session closes to the exact second.
06
Charged automatically
The stored payment method is billed for the exact duration. Funds settle to the authority same-day. No card, no cash, no ticket.
